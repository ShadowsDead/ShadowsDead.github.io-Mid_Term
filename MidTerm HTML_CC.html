My Hobbies

Traditional Art

Every so often I sit down to draw in a smooth sketch book usually for around 3 to 6 hours, usually its cute or creepy horror styled drawings that tend to have hints of what I'm dealing with at that moment of my life.

Digital Art

Most of the time I will take my traditional drawing to make them digital drawings, but there are times that I will work on pure digital drawings that usually take about the same time as a traditional drawing.

Graphic Design

Usually I do graphic design for school work but there are times that I do so for personal business stuff and or client work, depending on what kind of projects I'm working on will also determine what I'm creating.
